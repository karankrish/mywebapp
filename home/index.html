About Us

The mission of the Alumni Association of College of Engineering,Kidangoor, is to reach, engage and serve all alumni, present students by networking with one another to foster a life-long intellectual and emotional connection between the college and its graduates. The association serves the need of CEK alumni for leadership, voluntary commitment, goodwill, financial support, enhancing industry academic collaboration and communications including public relations.

Freshers Day 2017
